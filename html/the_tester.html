Welcome to Gryphon Music


Warwick Levey started The Gryphon Music Academy in 2007. He has worked as a professional musician since 2004. He started off as a sales person, part time music teacher and guitar tech at Paul Bothner Music in Plumstead before opening the school full time. Lessons are held in a small studio inside Paul Bothner Music Plumstead. jygdfjhdkm


Lessons are tailored specifically to each student. Regardless of style or level of proficiency a syllabus will be written with your goals in mind. If you're looking to play acoustic pop songs, blues, a few songs around the camp fire or learn to shred, you'll find what you're looking for at the Gryphon Music Academy.


The youngest age we'll start at is 6, and we've had students as old as 72. All you require is a guitar, passion for music and the desire to learn.


Lessons are all done on a one on one basis. By special request, groups of no more than 2 will be considered.


If you require a more structured approach the Trinity Rock School Syllabus can be taught.
