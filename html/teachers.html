Teachers


At the Gryphon Music Adacemy you can choose between two teachers. Both Warwick and Chad are experienced musicians
each with 9 years full time experience in the music industry and as teachers

Warwick Levey

Warwick started learning music at a young age. He started with recorder and penny whistle. In high school he tought a penny whistle class, he also went on to play clarinet and trumpet in the Plumstead High School wind band. At the age of 15 he started playing bass, and at 16 he was given his first guitar.



After High School he started work at Paul Bothner Music and he worked as a guitar specialist and guitar tech. It was during this time that he started teaching. Part time at first, and then olpening the school in 2007.



For the first few years he played classical guitar. He started to get bored with classical and ventured in blues and rock, and is currently playing in "Sorry Mr Devil", an acoustic blues duet.


STYLES

Classical, Rock, Blues, Pop, Folk, Metal and Contemporary



Warwick with Guitar Legand Guthrie Govan

Chad Browne


Chad started music young with piano at the age of 10, sadly it never really stuck and didn't progress too far until he discovered guitar at the age of 14, which he didn't really take seriously until the age of 16 upon receiving his first electric guitar and hearing Joe Satriani for the first time.


After this period he did a brief stint learning classical, but soon shifted focus to the styles of music his heros played, namely rock oriented shred guitar, as the years went on his tastes became more eclectic, likewise with the styles being incorporated into his playing, affording him much versatility, this versatility has gotten him a few lucky breaks including playing in a run of the successful theatre show "Rock Evolution" and also acting as a hired gun for a local pop act which afforded him the opportunity to play at the KKNK and a morning appearance on national T.V with the Expresso show. Chad is currently active as the guitarist in the progressive metal band Zero Stroke, and online creating ambient theme music under the handle GrimShark.


STYLES

Rock, Blues, Pop, Metal, Fusion, Gypsy Jazz, Shred, Progressive and Contemporary Acoustic.
